Why Stay With Us

  • Our guests love us:

    We have many loyal, repeat guests and are the #1 rated hotel in Behror

  • The best location in Behror:

    We are located on a quiet and romantic coastal perch (away from the traffic of Highway 8)

  • Oversized, comfortable rooms:

    All of our rooms have cozy down-comforter beds and private balconies and nearly all have memorable views

  • Locally owned:

    We are an independent, locally owned and operated hotel, and we take great satisfaction in making your stay on the coast wonderfully memorable.

  • Great local knowledge:

    Our friendly team has the best local knowledge and access to activities, excursions and adventures. Whether it's your first or your 50th visit to the Ganpati Hotel, we are pleased to assist you with reservations, great ideas and to arrange interesting itineraries.
